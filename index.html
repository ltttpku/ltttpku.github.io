I'm currently a PhD student at the Wangxuan Institite of Computer Technology (WICT) of School of Intelligence Science and Technology (WICT), Peking University, supervised by Prof. Yang Liu.

Prior to my PhD, I received a bachelor degree of Computer Science in Turing Class at the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science(EECS), Peking University.

My research interest is broadly in Computer Vision and Multi-Modal Learning, with particular interests in human-object interaction understanding currently. My research objective is to build an intelligent agent with the robust and generalizable ability to perceive and interact with a complex real-world environment.
